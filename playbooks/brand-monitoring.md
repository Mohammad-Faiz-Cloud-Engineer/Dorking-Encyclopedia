# Playbook — Brand Monitoring

> ⚠️ LEGAL WARNING
> Every technique in this file is for EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES ONLY.

Use dorking to monitor brand mentions, leaked credentials, or phishing sites referencing your company.

Phases:
1. Set up persistent Google searches for `site:` and product names.
2. Monitor GitHub for references to brand and credentials.
3. Use Pastebin and publicwww to find copied content.
